BICEP MUSCLE

biceps exercise is a physical activity that strengthens and tones the bicep muscles in the upper arm. Bicep exercises can be performed with dumbbells, barbells, or cables. .

TRICEPMUSCLE

Exercises such as push-ups, shoulder presses, dips and bench presses target the triceps. In other words, movements that involve upper body pushing and elbow extension will activate the triceps brachii. Body builders use isolated exercises to “target” the triceps; specifically, to train certain heads.

LEG

Fitness leg exercises are physical activities that strengthen and tone the muscles in your legs. They help improve your balance and stability, and can help correct muscle imbalances. .
